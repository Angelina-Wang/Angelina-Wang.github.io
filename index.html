My research is at the intersection of technology and society. My research statement (Spring 2024). Some themes I am currently interested in include AI fairness (FAccT 2022, PNAS 2025, Preprint 2025), evaluation (ICML 2021, Patterns 2024, Preprint 2025), and the societal impacts of AI (JRC 2023, Nature Machine Intelligence 2025). My work has been covered by outlets like Vice, Washington Post, New Scientist, and Tech Brew.
